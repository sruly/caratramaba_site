What We Do

Comprehensive ABA Therapy Programs

Each program is personalized, BCBA-supervised, and built around your child's unique strengths, needs, and family goals.

🏠
In-Home ABA Therapy

Our therapists come to your home, providing naturalistic learning in the environment where your child feels most comfortable and secure.

🏫
Center-Based Therapy

Structured sessions at our bright, welcoming Atlanta-area centers equipped with the latest learning tools and social skill building opportunities.

🤝
Social Skills Groups

Small-group sessions that build peer interaction, communication, and friendship skills in a fun, supervised, and encouraging environment.
